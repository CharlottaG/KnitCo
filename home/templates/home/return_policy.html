Returns & Reclamations

At Knit&Co, we strive to ensure your satisfaction with every purchase. If for any reason you are not completely satisfied with your order, we offer a straightforward return and reclamation process.

Returns

You may return unused and unopened items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Please contact our customer service team to initiate the return process.

Reclamation

In the rare event of receiving defective or damaged items, please notify us within 7 days of delivery. We will promptly replace the defective items or issue a refund as per your preference.

Conditions

Items must be returned in their original packaging and in new, unused condition. Returns are subject to inspection and approval before a refund or exchange is processed.

Contact Us

For any questions or concerns regarding returns or reclamation, please reach out to our customer service team at knitco.store@gmail.com
