Our Commitment to Sustainability

At Knit&Co, sustainability and ethical practices are at the heart of everything we do. We are dedicated to promoting sustainable practices throughout our operations. Here’s how we uphold our commitment:

Mulesing-Free Yarn:

We prioritize mulesing-free yarn, ensuring the welfare of animals in the production process.

Natural Fibers:

We exclusively offer yarn made from natural fibers, such as wool, cotton, and bamboo. These materials are biodegradable and renewable, reducing environmental impact.

Fair Working Conditions:

We support fair working conditions for all involved in our supply chain, from farmers to artisans. Respect and dignity are fundamental to our partnerships.

Local Resources:

We actively seek local resources to minimize transportation emissions and support our community. By using what’s close to us, we reduce waste and promote sustainable practices.

Join Us in Making a Difference:

Together, we can knit a brighter future. Explore our collection and discover how small choices can lead to significant changes for our planet and its people.

Thank you for choosing Knit&Co, where sustainability meets creativity.
