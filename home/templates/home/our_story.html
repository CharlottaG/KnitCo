Our Story - Woven with Tradition

Welcome to Knit & Co, your premier online yarn shop dedicated to preserving the rich tradition of Nordic knitting. Our mission is to bring you the finest selection of natural fiber yarns, sourced exclusively from Nordic producers who share our passion for quality, sustainability, and ethical practices.

Founded with a love for the intricate patterns and timeless techniques of Nordic knitting, Knit & Co was born out of a desire to keep these traditions alive and thriving. My personal journey with knitting began at the age of five, when I was taught by my grandmother. Those early years were filled with the warmth and love of learning to knit with her, creating a foundation that I cherish to this day. The legacy of knitting in my life continued when I inherited yarns, needles, and hooks from my husband's grandmother upon her passing. These treasured items, along with the yarn supply from my sister-in-law's grandmother, have become integral parts of my knitting practice. Each stitch I make carries forward the traditions and memories of these remarkable women, and it is this spirit of heritage and continuity that I bring to Knit & Co.

Our Commitment

At Knit & Co, we believe in doing business the right way. This means caring for the environment, ensuring fair trade, and maintaining good working conditions for all involved in the production process. Our yarns are made from natural fibers, chosen not only for their superior quality but also for their minimal environmental impact. We take pride in our commitment to ethical sourcing, and all our yarns are guaranteed to be mulesing-free, ensuring the welfare of the animals from which they are derived.

Why Choose Knit & Co?

- Nordic Focus - Our collection is dedicated to Nordic designs and producers, preserving the unique knitting traditions of the region.
- Natural Fibers - We offer yarns made from the finest natural fibers, providing you with high-quality, eco-friendly options.
- Ethical Practices - We are committed to fair trade, good working conditions, and the welfare of animals, ensuring a positive impact from farm to needle.
- Heritage and Legacy - Our yarns and knitting supplies carry the legacy of generations, honoring the past while creating for the future.

Join Us in Keeping Nordic Traditions Alive

Whether you are an experienced knitter or just starting your knitting journey, Knit & Co is here to inspire and support you with beautiful, sustainable yarns and timeless Nordic designs. Join our community of knitters who appreciate the artistry and heritage of Nordic knitting, and help us keep these cherished traditions alive for generations to come.

Thank you for choosing Knit & Co. Happy knitting!
